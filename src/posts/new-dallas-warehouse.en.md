# New Dallas Facility Now Operational

Loflog continues to invest in infrastructure to better serve our cross-border e-commerce clients. Our newest facility in **Dallas, Texas**, adds 50,000 square feet of smart warehousing space to our network.

## Strategic Location

Texas serves as a central hub, allowing us to reach the Midwest and South effectively.

- **Location**: North Dallas Logistics Park
- **Capacity**: 500,000 unit throughput per month
- **Technology**: Fully automated sorting and retrieval systems

![Warehouse Interior](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1590247813693-5541d1c609fd?ixlib=rb-1.2.1&auto=format&fit=crop&w=1000&q=80)

## Benefit to Clients

With this new node, our **Dropshipping** service now offers:

- **Lower Shipping Costs**: Zone skipping optimization.
- **Faster Delivery**: 1-2 day ground shipping to major Texas cities and neighbors.
- **Late Cutoff Times**: Orders placed by 4 PM CST ship same-day.

We are committed to helping your business grow by providing world-class logistics infrastructure.
